Building Homes for the Poor

Manny has funded the construction of thousands of homes for impoverished families in the Philippines, providing shelter and hope to those in need.

Medical Assistance Programs

He has also organized numerous medical missions to provide free healthcare services to underprivileged communities, helping those who cannot afford medical treatment.

Educational Support

Manny is passionate about education and has offered scholarships and financial aid to students from low-income families to support their studies.

Manny Pacquiao Foundation

The Manny Pacquiao Foundation is dedicated to empowering the underprivileged and providing essential services in various sectors such as health, education, and community development. Through this foundation, Manny aims to make a lasting impact on the lives of those in need.

The foundation focuses on providing food, shelter, and medical assistance to families and communities affected by poverty and natural disasters. Additionally, it offers educational resources and support to children, helping them to achieve their dreams and break the cycle of poverty.
