Career Highlights

2001 - International Breakthrough

Pacquiao's rise to international fame began with his victory over Lehlohonolo Ledwaba, where he captured the IBF Super Bantamweight title. This win marked the start of his global boxing career, showcasing his speed and power on an international stage.

2003 - Featherweight Victory Over Marco Antonio Barrera

In a fight that solidified his status as a boxing superstar, Pacquiao defeated Mexican legend Marco Antonio Barrera via technical knockout. This victory is often considered a turning point in Pacquiao's career, proving that he could compete against the best in the world.

2008 - Defeated Oscar De La Hoya in a Historic Fight

Pacquiao moved up in weight to face Oscar De La Hoya in what was dubbed the "Dream Match." Despite being the underdog, he dominated De La Hoya, leading to the fight being stopped after eight rounds. This victory established Pacquiao as a multi-division champion and a true pound-for-pound king.

2015 - Fight of the Century

The long-awaited showdown between Manny Pacquiao and Floyd Mayweather in 2015, one of the most highly anticipated fights in boxing history.
